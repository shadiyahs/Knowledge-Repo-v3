File naming convention

Accepted file formats

  • PPTX — presentations and pitch decks
  • DOCX — editable templates and playbooks
Writing a good summary
  • 2–3 sentences, 30–60 words
  • State what the content covers
  • State who it is most relevant for
  • Avoid internal jargon and acronyms
Publish-ready checklist
  • On the current Fortude brand template
  • No placeholder or draft text remaining
  • Spell-checked and internally reviewed
  • No outdated logos, colours or taglines
  • Client names cleared for external use
